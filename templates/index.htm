    About Us

    Our team is a group of second year undergraduates of Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi. Having Database Management Systems as our course, we are working on the event manager project.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to target the audience in need of managing some event and is finding it hard to keep record of each and everything going in the event and make a framework in order to make this task automated.

    Our Plan

    We plan to work on developing the template first, after which we will be testing it by populating it with dummy data to ensure it covers everything needed. Finally, we plan to get this working on a framework.

    Our Vision

    The vision may be simple to interpret as it is just to save the time of the manager of events, finding it hard to keep track of literally everything ongoing in an event.

    Features

    Eventer provides many high level functionalities that are required by any individual related in any way to a specific event. Following are the few of them.

    Organization

    Help event heads to organize their events systematically and efficiently by providing them with the data.

    Updation

    Control and update the ongoing event regularly and conveniently.

    Information

    Inform the participants and the audience to know the details of any ongoing event.
